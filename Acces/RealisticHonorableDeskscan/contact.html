Contact Us 📧

We're here to help with AccessScan related inquiries

⚠️ Before You Contact Us

Please understand that we can only assist with issues directly related to the AccessScan block explorer services.

⏳

Pending Transactions

We do not process transactions and cannot expedite, cancel, or replace them. If you have issues with pending transactions, please contact your wallet provider.

🔍

Block Explorer Only

AccessScan is an independent block explorer. We cannot provide support for wallet issues, exchange problems, or third-party services.

📚

Check Our FAQ

Your question might already be answered in our FAQ page. Please check there first.

👋

Community Support

We will NEVER ask for your private keys or passwords. Never share these with anyone claiming to be from AccessScan.

🏦

Wallet / Exchange Issues

Please contact your wallet provider or exchange directly for issues related to their platforms.
